To support voice searches on Android Auto, you should also register an intent-filter for the action android.media.action.MEDIA_PLAY_FROM_SEARCH. To do this, add <intent-filter> <action android:name="android.media.action.MEDIA_PLAY_FROM_SEARCH" /> </intent-filter> to your <activity> or <service>.

More information: https://developer.android.com/training/auto/audio/index.html#support_voice

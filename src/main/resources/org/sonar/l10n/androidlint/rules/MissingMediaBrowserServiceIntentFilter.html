An Automotive Media App requires an exported service that extends android.service.media.MediaBrowserService with an intent-filter for the action android.media.browse.MediaBrowserService to be able to browse and play media. To do this, add `<intent-filter>` `<action android:name="android.media.browse.MediaBrowserService" />` `</intent-filter>` to the service that extends android.service.media.MediaBrowserService

More information: https://developer.android.com/training/auto/audio/index.html#config_manifest
